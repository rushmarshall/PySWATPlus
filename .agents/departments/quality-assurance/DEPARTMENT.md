# Quality Assurance Department

## Mission
Ensure every deliverable meets the highest standards of scientific integrity, methodological rigor, and evidence quality before it leaves the institute.

## Agents
- **Scientific Reviewer** — Chief Scientific Officer; 5-phase cognitive review protocol covering reasoning quality, evidence evaluation, and narrative coherence
- **Citation Verifier** — Chief Verification Officer; DOI resolution, metadata validation, fabricated reference detection, and citation-claim alignment
- **Methodology Auditor** — Statistical and methodological validity checks, reproducibility assessment, and uncertainty quantification

## Handoff Protocols
- Receives manuscripts and analyses from all departments for review
- Scientific Reviewer coordinates Citation Verifier and Methodology Auditor
- Returns annotated feedback to originating department
- Escalates critical integrity issues directly to Research Director

## Quality Bar
- Every DOI must resolve via CrossRef with matching metadata
- No circular reasoning in any manuscript section
- All statistical methods must be appropriate for the data type and research question
- Temporal consistency verified across all claims
- No phantom citations (references that do not exist or do not support the claim)
- Self-improvement logs maintained after every major review
