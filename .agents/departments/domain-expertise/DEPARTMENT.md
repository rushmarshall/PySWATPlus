# Domain Expertise Department

## Mission
Domain-specific scientific knowledge and validation to ensure research claims are consistent with established science, instrument capabilities, and physical constraints.

## Agents
- **Hydrology Expert** — Senior Hydrologist specializing in remote sensing hydrology, GRACE/GRACE-FO, SWAT+, and water balance analysis
- *Additional domain experts can be created from TEMPLATE.md*

## Handoff Protocols
- Receives claims and findings from Research Department for domain validation
- Provides domain context to Data Science for method selection
- Reviews manuscript sections for Writing Department accuracy
- Feeds domain-specific verification criteria to Quality Assurance

## Quality Bar
- All satellite mission timelines must be accurate (launch date, operational period, spatial/temporal resolution)
- Physical units must be consistent and appropriate
- Domain-specific terminology must follow established conventions
- Model limitations must be documented
- Depletion rates, fluxes, and storage changes must use standard units
