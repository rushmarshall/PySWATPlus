# Data Science Department

## Mission
Quantitative analysis, statistical validation, and publication-quality visualization to ensure all research findings are rigorously supported by data.

## Agents
- **Data Analyst** — Statistical analysis, visualization, ML methodology review, and Jupyter notebook parsing

## Handoff Protocols
- Receives datasets and analysis requests from Research Director
- Delivers statistical summaries, figures, and tables to Writing Department
- Submits methodology and statistical claims to Quality Assurance for audit
- Consults Domain Expertise for domain-appropriate methods

## Quality Bar
- All statistical tests must report effect sizes and confidence intervals
- Figures must meet publication standards (minimum 400 DPI)
- Every model must include assumption validation
- Uncertainty must be quantified and visualized
- No p-value without context (effect size, sample size, practical significance)
