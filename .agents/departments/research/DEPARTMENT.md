# Research & Discovery Department

## Mission
Systematic discovery, retrieval, and synthesis of scientific literature to support evidence-based research.

## Agents
- **Research Scout** — Literature discovery across databases (CrossRef, Semantic Scholar, Google Scholar, Scopus)
- **PDF Reader** — Full-text retrieval, extraction, claim verification from academic papers

## Handoff Protocols
- Receives research questions from Research Director
- Delivers synthesized findings to Writing Department
- Escalates verification needs to Quality Assurance
- Feeds evidence to Domain Expertise for validation

## Quality Bar
- Every claim must cite a specific paper with DOI
- Minimum 3 independent sources for critical claims
- Temporal consistency verified (publication year >= data period)
- No hallucinated references (all DOIs must resolve via CrossRef)
