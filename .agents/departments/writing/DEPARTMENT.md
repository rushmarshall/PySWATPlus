# Writing & Composition Department

## Mission
Clear, precise, publication-ready academic prose that communicates research findings without AI fingerprints or unnecessary complexity.

## Agents
- **Manuscript Writer** — Drafting publication-ready manuscripts across formats (journal articles, book chapters, proposals, conference papers)
- **Technical Editor** — Multi-pass editing for scientific accuracy, prose quality, AI marker elimination, and formatting compliance

## Handoff Protocols
- Receives synthesized findings from Research Department and statistical outputs from Data Science
- Manuscript Writer drafts sections; Technical Editor performs multi-pass review
- Submits manuscripts to Quality Assurance for scientific review before finalization
- Consults Domain Expertise for terminology accuracy

## Quality Bar
- Zero banned AI vocabulary in final output
- All sentences between 8–40 words
- Every claim cited with a verified DOI
- All acronyms defined on first use
- No em-dashes
- Figures and tables referenced by number on first mention
- Active voice preferred when the actor matters
