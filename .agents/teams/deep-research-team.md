# Deep Research Team

## Purpose
Go beyond the manuscript's cited literature to identify missing context, competing findings, and strengthening opportunities.

## Research Questions
1. Are there major competing studies on drought recovery not cited? (e.g., Zheng et al., Deng et al.)
2. Is the ESMI index validated elsewhere? (Appears novel — is it published independently?)
3. Are there Nature manuscripts with similar framing that reviewers might compare?
4. What are the main counterarguments a Nature reviewer would raise?
5. Is the 2-month recovery threshold used by others or novel to this work?
6. How does this manuscript's 59% figure compare to global drought exposure estimates?

## Workflow
1. **pdf-reader** — Download and scan key competing papers
2. **drought-reviewer** — Assess gaps in methodological justification
3. **ultra-thinker** — Develop counterarguments and identify preemptive responses
